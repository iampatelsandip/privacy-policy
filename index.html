Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 27, 2025

Welcome to WhatsApp Status Saver & Tools (“the App”). Your privacy is important to us. We do NOT collect, store, or share any personal user data.

Information We Access

  • Media Files: To view, download, and share WhatsApp statuses, the App accesses photos, videos, and statuses stored on your device.
  • Notifications: To enable message recovery, the App may read WhatsApp notifications. Messages are never stored or shared.
  • Internet: Required for web-based features, WhatsApp Web integration, and advertisements.
  • Device Storage: Needed to save downloaded statuses and temporary app files.
  • The App does not access contacts, messages, location, or any other personal data without your explicit permission.

Permissions Used

  • Storage Permission: To access and save media files and statuses.
  • Notification Access: To recover statuses from WhatsApp notifications.
  • Internet Access: For web features and displaying ads.

Data Sharing

  • We do NOT share, sell, or distribute any personal user data.
  • The App may include ads from third-party providers (e.g., Google AdMob). These providers may collect anonymous data for advertising purposes only.

Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Compliance with Google Play Policies

  • All permissions required by the App are disclosed in the Play Store listing.
  • Users are fully informed about data access, and no personal data is collected without consent.
  • This privacy policy is publicly available and linked in the Play Store listing.

Changes to This Privacy Policy
